Supervisors Guide

Approving and Rejecting Students on a Module

If you are a supervisor you are able to approve and reject the requests from students under your supervision who have enrolled on a module. You will receive an email when any of your students enrol for a module asking you to approve or reject the request.

  1. When you receive an automated email, click on the link provided in the email which will take you into the Graduate Training tool.
  2. When a student has been ACCEPTED by the module administrator they will appear on the Module Administration page. Using the hyperlinks under Actions by each student's name you can change the status to APPROVE or REJECT.
  3. By clicking APPROVE the student will be sent an email informing them that they have been approved and are able to attend the course. By clicking REJECT they will be sent an email informing them that they have been rejected and are unable to attend the course.

Viewing Your Students Attending Modules

Supervisors are able to view a list of their students who are enrolled on courses throughout the Division.

  1. To view module attendance, click the Module Administration link at the top of the page.
  2. A list of students will appear and these can be modified using the Status Filter.
  3. You have the option of changing a student's status in this screen by using the drop down list next to each name.
